My team designed a automonous robot for a cube collection contest. I mainly worked on the codeing portion as well as some testing, and we managed to get to second place. Our main design was a 4 wheel driving robot that was low to the ground so that other robots would have difficulty moving it around in the arena. See the pdf below on the full overview as well as the analysis of our cube collection robot.
