For my reflection on which homework assignment I found the most valuable, I chose HW3, which is linked as a pdf below. HW3 was where the use of resistance networks came in, and the versatility of being able to represent a multi-step process consisting of not only conduction, convection, and even radiation as one connected equation proved to make the process of solving these problems not only easier, but also much faster. Resistance networks also simplifies my thought process by allowing me to see the heat transfer network as a circuit, which I already have some experience in. This concept doesn't go away either, since it has consistently shown up multiple times since the introduction of the topic in class and will likely continue to appear in the future. Thus, because HW3 taught me how to use this concept, I chose HW3 as the most valuable of the homework assignments done so far.
